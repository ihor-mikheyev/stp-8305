FAQ

  • Is Match Villains free to play?

    Yes! You can download and play for free, with optional in-app purchases available.

  • Can I play offline?

    Yes, most levels can be played offline, but certain events and leaderboards require an internet connection.

  • How do I unlock new characters?

    Complete missions, earn rewards, and recruit legendary villains to join your team.

  • Are there different game modes?

    Absolutely! Enjoy story-driven missions, time-limited heists, and challenging puzzle battles.
